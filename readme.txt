==Description==

When you compose a post, wrap the NSFW content in `[nsfw][/nsfw]` to hide it. Readers can click on the "Show" link to read the hidden content.

==Installation==

Unzip the zip file into the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

==Screenshots==

1. Wrap the NSFW content in `[nsfw][/nsfw]` when you compose a Post.
2. Readers can to click on the "Show" link to see the NSFW content.
3. Readers can hide the NSFW content again by clicking on the "Hide" link.
